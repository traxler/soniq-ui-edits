FAQ

The questions artists actually ask.

What's a fit score?

A 0–100 estimate of how well your release matches a specific playlist or outlet, built from the curator's recent adds, genre distribution, and how you describe your own sound. Every score comes with the reasoning in plain language, including when the honest answer is "skip this one."

What signals does Soniq read?

For public playlists: recent adds, update recency, follower counts, and genre distribution. For your release: your links, your one-line description, and the track itself if you upload it. We show you what we observe; we don't certify curators, and we'll tell you when the signal is thin.

Do you email curators for me?

No, and that's the point. Soniq drafts the pitch; you approve it and send it from your own inbox. Curators see a real email from a real artist, and the relationship stays yours.

Where do the curators come from?

You bring them. Paste the playlists and press you already have in mind, or search public playlists by genre or vibe. Soniq doesn't sell access to a contact database, and there's no pay-for-placement anywhere in the product.

What does it cost?

Fit scoring is unlimited and free on every plan. Your first three pitch drafts are free too; the paywall appears at the fourth. Solo is $19/mo for unlimited drafts, and Momentum is $39/mo for teams. No credit card to start.
