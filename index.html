FAQ

The questions artists actually ask.

What's a fit score?

A 0–100 estimate of how well your release matches a specific playlist or outlet, built from the curator's recent adds, genre distribution, and how you describe your own sound. Every score comes with the reasoning in plain language, including when the honest answer is "skip this one."

Do you email curators for me?

No, and that's the point. Soniq drafts the pitch; you approve it and send it from your own inbox. Curators see a real email from a real artist, and the relationship stays yours.
