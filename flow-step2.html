Design notes · Step 2

Design-system alignment

  • Tokens swapped to the DS values (Void Black canvas, Electric Purple, Laser Yellow); the flow card is a 16px large surface, result rows are 12px cards, genre chips are 4px Tags. Body is Figtree; the step label and captured line moved off JetBrains Mono, which is reserved for scores and timestamps. Purple focus rings on the input.
  • Entry surface, so the spotlights atmosphere stays (Rule 8). "Score the fit" is this screen's single Laser Yellow action; "Search playlists" is quiet, links are purple.

Review feedback applied

  • C1: result rows carry observable signals only (followers, track count, update recency, genre tags); no verified or unverified state exists anywhere in the flow. The pre-score note keeps the no-contact promise and drops the "verification comes later" language, since verification isn't a launch capability.
  • Adopted from the alpha punch list per the review: richer result metadata and the enabled-on-selection button pattern were already on the internal list; no changes requested.
