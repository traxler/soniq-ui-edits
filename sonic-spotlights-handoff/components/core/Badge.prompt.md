Status marker. `soft` (default) is a filled pill; `outline` is the mono uppercase hairline tag used on cards (UNVERIFIED, PRESS, YOU ADDED, DRAFT, BOUNCED).

```jsx
<Badge tone="positive" dot>In motion</Badge>
<Badge tone="inferred">Inferred</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline">Unverified</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline" tone="voice">You added</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline" tone="danger">Bounced</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | voice | positive | inferred | danger`.
