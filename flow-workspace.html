Design notes · Workspace ready
  • Name-the-release input added here. In the dashboard screenshots, "Your current release" leaks as a literal name into headings ("Where should Your current release go?"), the media library, and the "YO" avatar. Naming at the moment of workspace creation fixes every downstream screen and makes the workspace feel like theirs.
  • "Memory — started · learning your voice" read as vague and slightly surveillance-y. Renamed to "Voice profile" and tied it to a concrete user action (editing drafts), which also motivates the edit behavior you want.
  • "Trial active · no card on file" was the first mention of a trial anywhere in the product, which reads as a bait-and-switch signal. Replaced with the actual plan terms (free plan, 5 scores) and a pricing link. Pricing now also exists on the homepage, so this is a reminder, not a reveal.
  • "Open your preview draft" moved onto the pitch line it refers to, instead of floating below the checklist.
