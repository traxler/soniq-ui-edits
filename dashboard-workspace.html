Design notes · Workspace (targets)
  • Naming bug fixed at the source: "Where should Your current release go?" becomes "Where should Holding Pattern EP go?" via the naming step added at workspace creation. Never interpolate a placeholder object name into a sentence.
  • The search card in the screenshot carried five chips including "Apple Music · not configured here" and "Partner network — coming later." Negative-state and roadmap chips are noise at the moment of action; both moved out (settings and changelog respectively). What remains: two input modes and one quiet add-contact link.
  • Score rings are colored by band (green/amber/red) so the list is scannable without reading numbers. Filters for Verified only and Strong fit (70+) support the real workflow: build a shortlist, ignore the rest.
  • Rows gain the same metadata as the flow search (followers, recency, genre chips) plus the recommend/skip language from the fit reasoning. The list should feel like a ranked shortlist with opinions, not a table of numbers.
  • Batch selection bar (sticky) replaces the lone dim "Select a curator to draft" button. It states what's selected, summarizes quality (avg fit), and the CTA names the exact outcome: "Draft 2 pitches."
  • Sidebar footer shows the artist/project name (Grayline) instead of "Your project," same placeholder-leak fix.
