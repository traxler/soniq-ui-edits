ON THE HORIZON · THE CURATOR MODEL V7.4 · NEW

Three phases — from bring-your-own to a verified network.

● NOW · SHIPPING
PHASE 0 · MVP
Bring your own curator
Search or paste any playlist; Soniq scores fit and drafts each pitch in your voice.
Send: copy-paste / open in your own inbox. Soniq never sends.
Verification: none yet — every match is fit-only, honestly unverified.
Tracking: artist-logged — mark sent, log replies and placements.
▸ NEXT
PHASE 1 · PARTNER INTEGRATION
Verified send (e.g. Groover)
Suggested verified curators from the partner network — discovery beyond your own list.
Send: on-behalf, through the partner's own channel to curators who opted in.
Verification: the signal appears — fit and verified stay separate, never merged.
Tracking: automatic status + receipts on that path.
◇ LATER
PHASE 2 · OWNED CATALOG
A verified list we own
Curators request to be listed; Soniq vets and builds a proprietary catalog.
Send: consent is built in — a listed curator has invited the pitch.
Verification: the moat — a verified network Soniq owns, not rents.
Tracking: owned, end to end — no partner dependency.
CARRIES ACROSS ALL THREE
Fit and verification never merge — two independent signals. And the artist is always the sender until a partner channel earns that role. The lens doesn't change; the reach does.
