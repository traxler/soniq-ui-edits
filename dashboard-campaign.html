Design notes · Campaign detail

Design-system alignment

  • One-yellow violation fixed: this screen previously carried yellow on both "Open in email" and "Log outcome." "Open in email" in the Ready column is the single most important decision on the page and keeps the yellow; "Log outcome" is now secondary purple. StatusDot-style state chips, mono for scores and elapsed-time stamps, calm workspace with no atmosphere.
  • Rule 4/5 hold throughout: there is no Send button, no delivery or opened state anywhere. The board's Sent state is artist-declared via mark-as-sent, and replies are artist-logged. The explainer strip states this once so the board reads honestly.

Review feedback applied

  • The pipeline board, follow-up clock, snooze, reply-gist line, and the media-kit handoff from the Replied column were all adopted by the review as-is; this page is flagged as the missing core of the product and is heading for the build queue. The only requested change was the yellow fix above.
  • Coverage tracking sits on this page but bills under Momentum per the revised tiers; the empty state copy is unchanged.
